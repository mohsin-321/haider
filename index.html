Tolipir (Urdu: تولی پیر) is a hilltop area situated in Tehsil Rawalakot in
the Poonch District of Azad Kashmir. Its approximate elevation is about 8800 ft above sea level.
It is about 30 km, or a 120-minute drive, from Rawalakot in Azad Kashmir.[1] Abbaspur, Bagh and
Poonch River can be viewed from Toli Pir. Tolipir is the highest mountainous location in the northeastern area
of Rawalakot; it is the point of origin of three different mountain ridges. The tourist rest house
on the way to Toli Pir is also situated in a scenic location.[citation needed] There are some remains
of an old mazar on the highest hilltop.[2] Tolipir is most accessible during the summer months; the weather
generally becomes colder from October through March.[citation needed]

Ganga Choti (Urdu: گنگا چوٹی) is a peak near the Union Council Birpani village Bani Minhasan located in between Chikar ,a historical city in ancient times especially Dogra and Budshah dynasty and one of the most beautiful Bagh District of Kashmir administered by Pakistan.Its major part is in Bagh District and also has some part in Chikar District. It is located in Pir Panjal Range, at the elevation of 3,045 metres (9,990 ft).[2][3] Ganga Choti can be reached from two ways , one is from Chikar and the other is from Bagh Sudan Gali Road.
